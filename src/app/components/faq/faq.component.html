Planning permission take approximately 8 weeks from submission. Please note this time frame does not include drawings or any amendments required to get this project through. For much larger or complex project applications can take approximately 13 weeks.
Will I need building control?
Building control is required when constructing a new building or undertaking structural works or alterations to an existing building. We will inform you if your project required building control and advise what costs would be associated as part of our detailed quotation.
Do you provide building control?
We are associated with some of the leading building control firms in the country. However we do not provide building control as an independent party must undertake this.
